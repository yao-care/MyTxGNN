## Why So Many Predictions?

### 1. Broad Mechanism of Action

Corticosteroids like Prednisolone work through multiple pathways:
- **Anti-inflammatory**: Inhibits prostaglandin and leukotriene synthesis
- **Immunosuppressive**: Reduces lymphocyte activity
- **Anti-allergic**: Stabilizes mast cells

### 2. Knowledge Graph Position

In the TxGNN knowledge graph, Prednisolone is highly connected due to:
- Existing approvals for dozens of conditions
- Shared pathways with many inflammatory diseases
- Extensive clinical trial history

### 3. Disease Coverage

The 3,650 predictions span multiple therapeutic areas:
- Autoimmune diseases
- Inflammatory conditions
- Allergic disorders
- Dermatological conditions
- Respiratory diseases

## Key Learnings

<div class="key-takeaway">
High prediction count doesn't always mean new discoveries. For well-established drugs like Prednisolone, many predictions reflect existing clinical knowledge captured in the knowledge graph.
</div>

### What This Teaches Us

1. **Validation of KG approach**: High counts for known drugs validate the method
2. **Filter for novelty**: Focus on predictions that are NOT current indications
3. **Consider drug class**: Corticosteroids naturally have broad applicability

## Research Implications

### For Novel Indications

Among the 3,650 predictions, researchers should:
