# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

---

## Overview

MyTxGNN ("we", "us", "our") operates the MyTxGNN drug repurposing prediction platform (https://mytxgnn.yao.care). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our Service.

---

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

- **Log Data**: Browser type, pages visited, time spent, referring URL
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system
- **IP Address**: For security and analytics purposes

### Information You Provide

We do not require user registration. If you contact us:

- **Email Communications**: Your email address and message content
- **GitHub Interactions**: Public information from GitHub if you open issues

---

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Improve the Service**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality
2. **Security**: Detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access
3. **Communications**: Respond to inquiries when you contact us
4. **Analytics**: Understand how users interact with our platform

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

MyTxGNN does **not** collect:

- Personal health information
- Patient data
- Medical records
- Financial information
- Personal identification documents

---

## Third-Party Services

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites:

- **Harvard TxGNN**: Model and knowledge graph source
- **ClinicalTrials.gov**: Clinical trial data
- **PubMed**: Literature references
- **DrugBank**: Drug information
- **NPRA/data.gov.my**: Malaysia regulatory data
- **GitHub**: Issue tracking and discussions

These sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

---

## Cookies

We may use cookies for:

- **Analytics**: Understanding site usage (via privacy-respecting analytics)
- **Functionality**: Remembering user preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

---

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is hosted on GitHub Pages
- We use HTTPS for all connections
- No user databases are maintained
- All prediction data is publicly available

---

## Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request information about data we have about you
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of any personal data
- **Opt-out**: Disable cookies or analytics

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated date.

---

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
